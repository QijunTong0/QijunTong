I am a PhD student at the [University of Electro-Communications](https://www.uec.ac.jp/eng/), Tokyo, Japan (since October 2025). My research interests lie in **optimal transport theory**, **statistics**, and their applications to machine learning.

## Research Interests

- Entropy-regularized optimal transport
- Statistical theory on probability distributions (Gaussian processes, q-normal distributions)
- Mathematical optimization and its applications

## Background

Before starting my PhD, I worked as a **Data Scientist and Optimization Engineer** at [Accenture Japan Ltd](https://www.accenture.com/jp-ja) (June 2023 – present), where I led the development of advanced scheduling algorithms using mathematical optimization, deployed on AWS EKS.

Prior to that, I was a **Data Scientist and ML Engineer** at [ALBERT Inc.](https://www.albert2005.co.jp/) (April 2020 – May 2023), working on social media analysis, business intelligence systems, and NLP for customer complaint summarization.

I was also a part-time **Research Assistant** at the [RIKEN Centre for Advanced Intelligence Project (AIP)](https://www.riken.jp/en/research/labs/aip/) (April 2019 – March 2020), contributing to the Mathematical Science team.

I hold a Master of Engineering and Bachelor of Engineering from [Keio University](https://www.keio.ac.jp/en/).
